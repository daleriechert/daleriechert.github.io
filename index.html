Hello, I am Dale Riechert. I am currently studying Informatics at Arizona State University with a focus on digital culture. I plan to graduate in 2016. Informatics is an engineering field that creates connections between people, computers, and information. As such, informatics is incredibly diverse and can be applied nearly anywhere. Digital culture encompasses the culture we live throughout our lives today: surrounded by the aid of computers, and digital media. I previously studied computer science for three years but found informatics more enjoyable with its emphasis on practical application instead of theory. My passions include the video game and technology industries, as well as graphic design, foreign culture, and more.
I am currently interested in any internship and entry-level positions that will provide real world experience that will complement my coursework.
