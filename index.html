Hello, I am Dale Riechert. I am currently studying Informatics at Arizona State University with a focus in digital culture.

Everyone I meet just nods their head when I say informatics, or are honest and have to ask. Informatics is a newer designation for the engineering field that simply creates connections between people, computers, and information. As such, informatics is incredibly diverse and can be applied nearly anywhere. Digital culture encompasses the culture we live throughout our lives today: surrounded by the aid of computers, and digital media.

My passions include the video game and technology industries, but I also hold interest in graphic design, foreign culture, film, and more.

To the left you can find some of the things I have been working on, with more to be added in time. I am currently interested in any internship and entry-level opportunities that will provide real world experience where I can apply everything that I have learned.
