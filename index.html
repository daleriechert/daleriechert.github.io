Hello, I am Dale Riechert, a student studying informatics and digital culture at Arizona State University.

Informatics is an interdisciplinary field incorporating areas such as computer science, information systems, and human computer interaction. I am what comes between data, computers, and people. As we head deeper into the 21st century these computer systems are playing an ever bigger role, becoming essential to nearly every other field out there. Digital culture is the study of digital media and the digital systems in our every day life, bringing creative solutions to today and tomorrow's problems.

My passions include the video game and consumer technology industries. I also hold interests in graphic design, photography, foreign culture, film, and more. As a hobby I like to collect, mostly Russian mechanical, wristwatches.

Motion Control Drum Machine

This is an interactive drum machine using an Arduino and potentiometers for note adjustment, a Wii remote and GlovePIE for motion control, and Max for note processing and system linkage. Each time the user swings the remote, in order indicated by the LEDs, one of the six notes will play.
