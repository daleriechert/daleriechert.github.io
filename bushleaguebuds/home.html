Who We Are

Bush League Buds began growing its roots nearly three decades ago at the beginning of the “Medicinal Era” in Everett, WA. It was founded by a couple of buddies from high school to help one of them manage the chronic pain he suffers from a career as a laborer, electrician. Disappointed in the potency of the market offerings at that time, these local boys committed to growing a strain unique to them and as potent, or more so, than all the other “Top Shelf” producers. Consistently testing at 28% THC in the early years, Bush League Buds is excited to introduce its special blend to the new era of cannabis consumers.

Bush League Buds is dedicated to producing and processing a “Top Shelf” flower, at a price everyone can afford. Whether you imbibe recreationally, or consume to manage pain or anxiety, we at BLB believe you deserve the best!

Thank you for supporting Bush League Buds, proud supporters of our military veterans.
